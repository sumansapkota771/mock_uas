About UAS Mock Exam System

Your comprehensive preparation platform for International UAS entrance examinations

What is the International UAS Exam?

The International UAS Exam is a joint digital entrance exam of the Finnish Universities of Applied Sciences (UAS), used for selecting students to study programmes leading to a bachelor's degree and conducted in English. The entrance exam is completed by the applicant's own computer remotely online.

Exam Structure

The exam consists of 6 main sections:

Reasoning Skills
25 minutes • Logical reasoning and problem-solving
English Language Skills
20 minutes • Reading comprehension and language skills
Mathematical Skills
25 minutes • Basic mathematics and calculations
Advanced Mathematical Skills
30 minutes • Advanced math and physics (Technology field)
Ethical Skills
10 minutes • Ethical reasoning and decision-making
Emotional Intelligence
10 minutes • Understanding and managing emotions
